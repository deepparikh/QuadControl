Re: Flashing Error and ESC gets hot

by JussiH » Tue Oct 16, 2012 1:17 pm

Do you have the battery ground hooked directly to FDTI ground? That can possibly create a gound-loop and explain why the ESC gets hot. Try and flash, just using a FDTI-cable and supplying 5V from that.

Has the ESC worked previously, or was this the first time you tried it? If its dud, it will be replaced!

What kind of battery power are you supplying to the ESC? If its more than 3S, the LDO will heat up considerably when stepping down >12V to 3.3V
